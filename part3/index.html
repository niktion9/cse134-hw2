About the QED-3000

The QED-3000 is a fictional productivity system designed to help users organize tasks, choose coffee, and complete ambitious projects. The abbreviation QED represents its central goal.

This version includes an improved focus mode and requires twelve cups of coffee per day.

Productivity is not about doing everything. It is about deciding what deserves your attention.
